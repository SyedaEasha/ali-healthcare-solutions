FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does credentialing take?

Credentialing timelines vary by insurance network and state, but typically range from 60 to 120 days. We work to expedite the process by ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate at submission, reducing back-and-forth delays. We'll give you a realistic timeline estimate during your free consultation.

Do you work with Medicaid?

Yes — Medicaid enrollment and billing is one of our core specialties. We work with state Medicaid portals across the USA and understand the specific requirements for home care and NEMT Medicaid programs, including waiver programs, EVV compliance, and prior authorization workflows.

Do you support NEMT providers?

Absolutely. Non-Emergency Medical Transportation is one of our focus areas. We assist NEMT providers with Medicaid enrollment, broker credentialing (MTM, LogistiCare/Modivcare, etc.), billing, and scheduling support.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

We follow strict HIPAA-compliant workflows for all data handling. This includes encrypted file transfers, secure communication channels, signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all clients, and regular staff training on privacy and security protocols. Your patients' data is always protected.

What states do you work in?

We work with home care and NEMT agencies across all 50 states. Our team is experienced with the varying Medicaid and credentialing requirements by state, so we can guide you regardless of where your agency operates.

How do you charge for your services?

We offer flexible pricing depending on the services you need — project-based pricing for one-time setup services like credentialing, and monthly retainer options for ongoing billing and operational support. We'll provide a custom quote after your free consultation.
